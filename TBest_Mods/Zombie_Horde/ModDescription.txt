This Mods can make Zombies!

Zombies is a 'survival' game, in which the last standing humen player/team wins. 
This is accoplished by having the AI surrendering when there is one humen player/team left.

It is played mainly in two forms. Either Zombie-AI vs One Humen Team or ZombieAI + Humen FFA.

Choose how many AI zombies, how big starting stacks,number of starting bonuses and income from the starting bonus.
